Figure Skating Association Northern Territory

(FSANT)

About Us

The FSANT is the Northern territory's first association representing figure skating as sport in the NT.We hope to allow NT skaters to compete in interstate competition under FSANT membership.

Membership

FSANT hope to allow NT skaters to compete in interstate competition by becoming members. By signing up you can become the members and get the FSANT's membership.

Events

FSANT will give information about previous or upcoming events, such as local or interstate figure-skating competitions that have FSANT competing skaters.
